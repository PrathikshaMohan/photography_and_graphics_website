About Us

White art is a minor level graphic designing & photography organization based in Sri Lanka. White art provides creative service that ranges from graphic designing, video production to various other creative services that are highly valuable to its customers. Also we specialize in photography, corporate, family and senior portraits, often traveling to your destination to capture the perfect moment in the perfect place. We will be there with you every step of the way to guarantee your special moments are captured for all time. Please take a few minutes to get to know us. We would love to visit with you by phone or in person and talk about your event, portrait session or video project. Please reach out to us through the Contact Page.
